About me

I'm Ian Kehayes, a 3D Lighting Artist and Compositor with over a decade of experience building worlds from vertices and light. From child focussed 3D animation, to live action, I approach every project with the same goal: to make the viewer feel something they can't quite explain.

I am proficient in several softwares and disciplines that I have picked up over the years.
